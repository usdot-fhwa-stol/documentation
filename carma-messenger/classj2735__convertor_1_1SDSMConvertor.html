Detailed Description

Is the class responsible for converting J2735 SDSMs to CARMA usable SDSMs.

Handles conversion between SDSMs in the j3224_v2x_msgs and carma_v2x_msgs packages with additional conversions for j2735_v2x_msgs. Unit conversions and presence flags are also handled
