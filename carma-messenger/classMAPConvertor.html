Detailed Description

Is the class responsible for converting J2735 Maps to CARMA usable Mapss.

Handles conversion between Map messages in the j2735_msgs and cav_msgs packages. Unit conversions are handled Note: The concept of map Zoom is not accounted for in these conversions
